Conference Theme

Machine Learning Driven Technology

The first International Conference on International Conference on Advances in Wireless Technologies, Intelligent Computing and System Design. WIComSD-2022, will be held at the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research, Neelambur, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India, on 24th June and 25th June 2022.

Machine Learning (ML), one of the potential areas of research in artificial intelligence (AI), challenges the functioning of every field with its innumerable possibilities, and offers powerful and promising solutions to several real-world complexities. Research on Machine Learning, therefore, has significantly gained momentum and become an object of discussion in several intellectual forums to enhance the quality of research and establish resilience in the vast expanse of the earth. Observing the trajectictories and developments in the field specified above, the conference theme demands a dialogue to explore the unexplored avenues in the field and map the future possibilities and challenges. WIComSD-2022 conference will provide a pivotal interdisciplinary platform for bringing together scientists, scholars, innovators, and industry experts from various organizations across the world to discuss and disseminate the latest trends, state-of-the-art research findings, path-breaking ideas, and potential challenges in the thrust areas of RF & microwave components, circuits and systems; communication and networking technologies, intelligent learning, machine intelligence-based techniques; applications in deep learning and other related disciplines. The conference will feature a comprehensive technical program including keynote speeches, plenary sessions and tutorials by eminent persons from industry, academia, and research organizations.
